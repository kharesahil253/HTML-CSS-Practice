Pay per click (PPC) advertising is a popular form of digital advertising where advertisers pay each time someone clicks on their ads. PPC advertising is a great way for businesses to drive traffic to their website, increase brand awareness, and generate leads and sales. There are several types of PPC advertising, each with its unique features and benefits.

HERE ARE SOME OF THE MOST COMMON TYPES OF PPC ADVERTISING:

Search advertising: This is the most common type of PPC advertising, where advertisers bid on specific keywords related to their business. When someone searches for those keywords, the advertiser’s ad will appear at the top of the search engine results page (SERP).

Display advertising: This type of PPC advertising involves displaying ads on websites, apps, and social media platforms. Display ads can be in the form of text, images, or videos and can be targeted to specific audiences based on demographics, interests, and behaviors.

Social media advertising: This type of PPC advertising involves promoting ads on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn. Social media ads can be highly targeted and can be used to increase brand awareness, drive website traffic, and generate leads and sales.

Remarketing advertising: This type of PPC advertising involves targeting people who have already interacted with your website or ads. Remarketing ads can be used to remind people of your products or services and encourage them to make a purchase.
